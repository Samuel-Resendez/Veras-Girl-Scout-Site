About Me

Girl Scouts

I am a 16-year-old sophomore at Davis Senior High School. I have been a member for Girl Scout Troop #347 for a little over 10 years, composed of a group of girls whom I met in preschool. As a troop we enjoy eating cookies (strictly Girl Scout of course), volunteering, and frequenting the great outdoors.

My Life

I have lived in Davis for as long as I can remember with two loving parents and my older brother Sam. Aside from Girl Scouts, my hobbies include field hockey, baking, and playing with the newest addition to my family, a 10-week-old McNab named Luna. I hope I will someday get to travel to Africa, and my biggest idol growing up was always Usain Bolt. I also work for the Davis Enterprise, and would love to be an engineer one day.
